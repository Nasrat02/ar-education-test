📚 Educational AR Learning

Point your camera at markers to see 3D educational content

How to Use:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Print the "Hiro" marker or use a marker image
  3. Point your camera at the marker
  4. Click different lesson buttons to see various AR content
  5. Watch the 3D models rotate and interact!
